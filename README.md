## Hi there, I'm Adán Silva Flores 👋

I am a biologist with an M.Sc. in Ecology interested in understanding ecosystem processes across spatial scales by integrating microbial ecology, bioinformatics, environmental data science, and geospatial analysis.

My work combines field ecology, experimental research, and computational approaches to study plant–soil interactions, microbiomes, ecosystem restoration, and biodiversity conservation in terrestrial and coastal ecosystems.

## 🔬 Current Work

I am building an open and reproducible research workflow that integrates ecology, microbiology, bioinformatics, and geospatial analysis.

Current projects focus on:

- Ecological data analysis in Python and R
- Bioinformatics workflows for microbiome research
- GIS and remote sensing applications
- Open science resources and educational materials
- Scientific outreach and reproducible research
